SAN DIEGO \u2014 If you have been putting off getting your car washed, Thursday might be a good day to get it done.<\/p>\n

Soapy Joe\u2019s Car Wash will be giving free car washes to every car that comes to their new Convoy Street location in Kearny Mesa.<\/p>\n

The Convoy Street car wash, Soapy Joe\u2019s 20th location to open, will be giving out a free Magic Joe Wash to every customer, a $20 value.<\/p>\n

<\/aside>\n

Sticking with the 20 theme, every 20th car to go through the wash Thursday will also receive a free annual Magic Joe membership, a $385 value.<\/p>\n
