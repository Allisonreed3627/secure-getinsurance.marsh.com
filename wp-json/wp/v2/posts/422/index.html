Disneyland is considered hallowed ground for fans of Tiki culture who make annual pilgrimages in Hawaiian shirts, straw hats and puka shell necklaces to sip mai tais at Trader Sam\u2019s and visit the crowning glory of the kitsch movement \u2014 Walt Disney\u2019s Enchanted Tiki Room.<\/p>\n

Disneyland\u2019s Tiki history stretches from the Enchanted Tiki Room attraction, Tahitian Terrace restaurant and Don the Beachcomber food stand to the Trader Sam\u2019s Enchanted Tiki Bar cocktail lounge, Tropical Hideaway grab-and-go dining area and Tikiland Day annual event.<\/p>\n
