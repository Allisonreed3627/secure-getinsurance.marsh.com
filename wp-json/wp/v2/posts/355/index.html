SAN MARCOS, Calif. \u2014 A new 100-unit affordable housing complex opened Wednesday in the San Marcos area.<\/p>\n

Alora, located at 604 Richmar Avenue, consists of one-, two- and three-bedroom units for households earning 30 \u2013 50% of the area median income, according to the County of San Diego.<\/p>\n

<\/aside>\n

Some of the features residents will be able to take advantage of include exclusive access to a community building that houses a pool, barbeque area and bike parking, a separate basketball hoop area and several tot lots found throughout the complex for families with children.<\/p>\n

I\u2019d like to thank all of our Alora partners for helping people dream, strive and thrive \u2013 because without them, we wouldn\u2019t be this successful.\u201d<\/p>\n

On-site services are also available such as computer training and resume building resources and programs dedicated to health and wellness, financial literacy and parenting. Community-building activities such as holiday events will be organized by a service coordinator.<\/p>\n
