SAN DIEGO \u2014 An\u2019s Dry Cleaning<\/a> is being recognized as one of the top 10 best indie ice cream shops across the U.S.<\/p>\n

The North Park ice cream shop, located at 3017 Adams Avenue, ranks No. 2 on the list compiled by USA TODAY\u2019s 10Best travel website.<\/p>\n

Some gelato flavors on the menu include fig and spicy candied pecans, matcha and white chocolate, raspberry, mango and mint, blue chamomile, blackberry jam, among others.<\/p>\n

<\/aside>\n

Built in 1934, An\u2019s Dry Cleaning pays homage to the building\u2019s history.<\/p>\n

\u201cWe immediately fell in love with the location, the community, and the people. But changing the name of an old establishment, in an even older building, didn\u2019t feel right. So, we decided to keep it. The ice cream shop says it prides itself making gelato from scratch and combining quality ingredients with uncompromising processes to craft fresh and well-balanced gelato.<\/p>\n
